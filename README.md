The project bridges the gap between personal observation and empirical scientific evidence, establishing a strong narrative thread to validate the "Grey Sky" phenomenon.Personal MotivationThe project was sparked by a "culture shock" experience after moving to Ireland. Witnessing constant blue skies led to a reflection on the persistent haze and declining visibility back home in Maharashtra. This study investigates whether those visual observations match underlying scientific PM2.5 data, mapping the shift from a historical baseline to a hazardous structural reality.Key Technical ImplementationsData Triangulation: Combines NASA SEDAC Satellite-Derived Grid Data (for 25 years of historical temporal depth) with OGD Platform India Station Data (for current ground-truth precision).Spatial-Temporal Synthesis: Developed a novel GIS process to convert raw NASA raster coordinate points into Simple Features, dynamically clipping them to Maharashtra’s administrative boundaries using CRS 4326.GIS Workaround: Implemented a manual server-pull method via ne_download() to bypass broken library dependencies (rnaturalearthhires), ensuring stable deployment.Reactive Optimization: A unified reactive engine that synchronizes a single year-slider across five distinct statistical and spatial visualizations.Visualization FeaturesNational GIS Map: Employs an AQI-standard divergent color scale for instant health-risk interpretation of pollution "plumes."Maharashtra Deep-Dive: Includes localized GIS mapping and a Comparative Bar Chart (Maha vs. National) to establish regional inequality.Statistical Spread: Features alpha-blended boxplots with jittering to reveal hazardous outliers (60+ $\mu g/m^3$) that are typically obscured by state-level averages.Pollution Density Plots: Illustrates the "Statistical Signature" and the "long tail" of probability for extreme pollution exposure.How to UseNational Tab: Use the slider to view the 25-year expansion of pollution across the subcontinent.Maharashtra Tab: Examine localized hotspots and the specific statistical distribution of the state's air quality.National Stats: Compare any selected state's "pollution signature" against the national baseline.Reference ListVan Donkelaar, A. et al.
